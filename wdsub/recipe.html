PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES

  1. Necessary Items

    • Mixing bowl
    • Spoon or fork
    • Nonstick Pan

  2. Ingredients

    • Peanut Butter
    • Egg
    • Sugar

  3. Mixing

    • One cup of peanut putter into bowl.
    • Put one cup of sugar in bowl.
    • Crack one egg into bowl.
    • Stir ingredients until it is consistant.

  4. COOK

    • Preheat Oven to 360 degrees.
    • Use nonstick pan and take a small portion from the mix.
    • Roll it into a small ball and place in a order on the pan.
    • Place the pan into the oven and set a timer for 10 minutes and take out when fully cooked.

  5. ENJOY!!!
